Chocolate Brownie

Preparation time: 30 minutes | Serves: 4

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix sugar, butter, and eggs.
  3. Add cocoa, flour, salt, and baking powder.
  4. Pour into greased pan and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  5. Let it cool and serve.
